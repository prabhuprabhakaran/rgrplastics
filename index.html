About R G R Plastics

Established in 1989, R G R Plastics has made a name for itself in the list of top plastic container manufacturer/seller in Tamilnadu, India. The company is located in Kumbakonam, Tamilnadu, India.

R G R Plastics is one of the leading sellers of HDPE, LDPE, PP plastic products all over Tamilnadu. It is a quality driven organization offering a wide range of plastic injection mould, plastic blow mould products in a affordable price within preset time limit.

R G R Plastics is listed in Trade India's list of verified sellers offering supreme quality and bulk quantity.
