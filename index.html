About R G R Plastics

K R Govindarajan started R G R Plastics as a small company in 1989 at Kumbakonam, Tamilnadu, India. He faced a tough competition to sell his good quality product in the market. He strated manufacturing with small plastic containers using manual and semi automatic machines. Over the year with his hard effort and quality, He developed the company with fully automated machines also reached to more customers.

Veeramanikandan son of K R Govindarajan joined his father and started involving in the business on part time basis from 2010 after completing schooling and started full time concentration after his graduation from 2014. His Vision is to improve the company to next level with his innovative ideas.

Now, R G R Plastics is one of the leading sellers of HDPE, LDPE, PP plastic products all over Tamilnadu. R G R Plastics has made a name for itself in the list of top plastic container manufacturer/seller in Tamilnadu, India. It is a quality driven organization offering a wide range of injection mould, blow mould plastic products in a affordable price within preset time limit. We have rich experience in blow and injection mould structures. R G R Plastics is listed in Trade India's list of verified sellers offering supreme quality and bulk quantity.
